Tarsus IV

[Solatarius Prime]

Imperial Classification: Hive World

Sub-Classification: Desert World

A rising Imperial planet in the Solatarius system, Tarsus IV's hive cities have grown rapidly in the past centuries. Industry rapidly overtakes the desert wastelands. Rich Houses grow complacent and fat on excess. Chaos plots and sinister deals with xenos writhe just beneath the surface.

Tamarak Ridge

[Strategic Point]

Held by: no faction

Occupying Force: none

Tamarak Ridge is a natural formation. Its significant elevation gives an occupying force's sensor arrays commanding vision for thousands of kilometers.

Salt Flats Research Facility

[Research Base]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Tarsus Defense Force

The Salt Flats are the location of one of Tarsus IV's most prominent military development facilities. This is the base of production for the Tarsus Defense Force's armament as well as various heavy industrial machinery for the planet.

Special Rules: "Battlezone: Industrial World"

Hive Rasmus

[Hive City]

Held by: no faction

Occupying Force: Underhive Gangers

Hive Rasmus is one of the planet's smaller hive city. Its underhive still overflows with the children of Hive Aepen refugees from the uprising generations ago. Underhive gangers practically run he city and the Adeptus Arbites can do naught but perform token acts of policing as their requests for support from Hive Tarson fall on deaf ears.

Special Rules: "Cities of Death: Infestation"

Hive Tarson

[Hive City]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Tarsus Defense Force

Hive Tarson is the largest and capital city on Tarsus IV. The upper classes live in the high towers, insulated and out of touch with the growing turmoil. The underhive, however, is alive with crime ranging from petty theft to daemonic cults.

Special Rules: "Cities of Death"

Fuller Gap

[Strategic Point]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Tarsus Defense Force

The gap is a critical point for commerce across the vast equatorial desert. Nearly every trade route between hemispheres runs through this location. An army occupying this location will have a vice grip on the logistics of Tarsus IV.

Hive Sorrel

[Hive City]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: 73rd Scathian Guard

Sorrel is the second largest hive city on Tarsus IV. Its status is almost identical to Hive Tarson, but rife with corporate corruption rather than political.

Special Rules: "Cities of Death"

Rogain Outpost

[Military Base]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Tarsus Defense Force

Rogain is the largest military base on the planet. Home to the Tarsus Defense Force, it sports impressive defensive design. Design learned through nearly a century of rebellion 400 years ago.

Hive Aepen Ruins

[Ruins]

Held by: Chaos

Occupying Force: Angels of Sedition

Here lie the abandoned ruins of Hive Aepen, torn apart by a rebel uprising 400 years ago. It became home to the underbelly of Tarsus IV, a hiding place for renegades and heretics.

Special Rules: "Cities of Death: Pollution"

Uzion

[Solatarius]

Imperial Classification: Death World

Sub-Classification: "Jungle World"

Deadly jungle covers the surface of Uzion. Lithe jungle creatures, giant insectoids, and massive predatory birds tear apart Imperial subjects that dare to venture into its heart. Millions of poisonous plants and animals, sweltering heat, and lack of fresh water make for a doubley punishing environment. For almost six centuries Uzion has raised regiment after regiment of Imperial Guardsmen, fulfilling their tithe with men and women born and bred in a world saturated with death.

The Depths

[Strategic Point]

Held by: Drukhari

Occupying Force: Kabal of the Pale King

A dark, mysterious, and largely unexplored segment of the Uzion jungle. Filled with deadly predators, hazardous terrain, and reported alien artifacts similar to the ones found on Ilias. Many explorers have ventured to the xenos ruins in search of riches, none have returned.

Special Rules: "Cataclysm in Catacombs"

Outpost Ziggy

[Military Base]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: 124th Uzanite Rifles

Outpost “Ziggy” is so named to honor the heroic actions of a sergeant in a long forgotten campaign.

Special Rules: "Jungle in Flames"

Outpost Garia

[Military Base]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: 124th Uzanite Rifles

Outpost “Garia” is a base deep within the jungle of Uzion, known as one of the most dangerous training grounds due to the hostility of the native flora and fauna. It is the crucible from which the Uzanite guard draws it’s most stubborn troops.

Special Rules: "Jungle in Flames"

Uzon City

[Settlement]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: 73rd Scathian Guard

A rapidly developing city once abuzz with activity is now far quieter as civilians take shelter in their homes, trusting the newly garrisoned Scathian Guard to keep the region safe.

Special Rules: "Cities of Death"

Port Trep

[Starport]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: 124th Uzanite Guard

Port Trep was originally a trading port essential to the worldwide economy of Uzion. With the advent of the Uzanite Guard, it was put under military command, ferrying soldiers and munitions as opposed to food and luxuries.

The Paramarsh

[Strategic Point]

Held by: Drukhari

Occupying Force: Kabal of the Pale King

An outlying segment of the deep marsh, this area is home to many rural settlements. Despite intervention to instill the Imperial truth, legends persist of night attacks by an otherworldly and terrifying force.

Special Rules: "Jungle in Flames"

The Burrow

[Mine]

Held by: Chaos

Occupying Force: Emperor's Children

The Burrow was rapidly overtaken by warp influence, turning the mining tunnels into a network of flesh coated passages akin to a throat. Horrid screams and dirges emerge from these unnatural structures that can be heard for miles.

Special Rules: "Cataclysm in Catacombs"

Atlas Base

[Military Base]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: 124th Uzanite Guard

Home of the stalwart Uzanite Guard regiment, Atlas Base serves as the training grounds and staging point of many Imperial campaigns. Legendary in the eyes of the native populace, the site attracts hundreds of thousands of aspiring recruits each year.

Special Rules: "Jungle in Flames"

Ilias

[Solatarius]

Imperial Classification: Forge World

The barren planet of Ilias has long served the Imperium. Adeptus Mechanicus forges manufacture warp engines for the Imperial navy, casting sacred rituals to appease the engine's fickle machine spirits. Ilias's secondary role is as the fiefdom for the oldest Knight House in the sector: the Titan Legio Solitarum I. These massive manufactoriums have fueled the devastating and incredible weaponry of Imperial Knights for millenia.

Complex-12

[Strategic Point]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Adeptus Mechanicus

Complex-12 was a base of operations used by the Adeptus Mechanicus to analyze curious archaeotech specimens unearthed through excavation.

Special Rules: "Sector Mechanicus"

Astor Base

[Research Base]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Tarsus Defense Force

Astor Base is the pumping heart of Ilias. Its central location has it see traffic and activity from the Adeptus Mechanicus, Ilias Defense Force, and the Titan Legio Solitarum I. All logistics on the planet go through this hub.

Special Rules: "Sector Mechanicus"

Midlothian

[Hive City]

Held by: no faction

Occupying Force: Underhive Gangers

“Midlothian” is the name given to this remote location on Ilias. Few have reason to go here, but long ago Adeptus Mechanicus techpriests found evidence of advanced xenos technology. The area was cleansed, and local legend stopped anyone from investigating.

Data Center

[Hive City]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Tarsus Defense Force

A network of cogitator banks forms the Data Center of the Adeptus Mechanicus. The barren nature of the planet once allowed the tech priest garrison to pray over their machines in peace, although this is a luxury afforded no longer.

Special Rules: "Sector Mechanicus"

Port Olander

[Strategic Point]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Tarsus Defense Force

Port Olander is the only port on the small planet of Ilias. Its spaceports see heavy use during times of war.

Complex-08

[Hive City]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: 73rd Scathian Guard

This Adeptus Mechanicus forge complex was outfitted as the private manufactorum for the Titan Legio Solitarum I.

Special Rules: "Sector Mechanicus"

Ilias Proper

[Military Base]

Held by: Imperium

Occupying Force: Serfs of the Legio Solatarum

The Noble House of the Solitarum Knights is guarded with much vigilance by the House’s personal engines of war. The lush gardens and sculptures that once decorated the beautiful estates have been left in disrepair as their caretakers are all off at war.

Tallas

[Ruins]

Held by: Chaos

Occupying Force: Angels of Sedition

The ancient ruins of Tallas are now home to a webway portal created by Aeldari warp spiders. What the xenos seek on the ancient planet of Ilias is unknown...
